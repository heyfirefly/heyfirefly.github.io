The problem

Non-technical users often struggle with everyday computer tasks.

A simple task can turn into tabs, settings, downloads, passwords, popups, and words that do not explain what to do next.

  • Too many steps for something that should be simple.
  • Unclear language that makes people hesitate.
  • The constant fear of clicking the wrong thing.

Modes

Choose how Firefly helps.

Choose between two modes that help you achieve what you want.

Guide me

Firefly walks you through each step clearly, so you stay in control while learning what to do.

Handle it for me

Give permission and Firefly can safely complete the task for you, without the confusing back and forth.
